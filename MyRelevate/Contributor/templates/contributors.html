Meet Our Contributors

Our contributors at Relevate are scholars and practitioners with expert knowledge to share about romantic relationships. Our contributors range from University professors, graduate students, certified therapists and relationship educators, and undergraduate students studying relationships under mentorship. Each contributor has been vetted by the Relevate team to ensure that all content is trustworthy and grounded in romantic relationship theory and research in order to ensure that everyone has access to the cutting edge knowledge and skills they need to elevate current and future romantic relationships.
