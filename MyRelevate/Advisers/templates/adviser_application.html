Adviser Application


MyRelevate advisors provide guidance and feedback to contributors who have not yet attained a graduate degree on the quality of the content they create for MyRelevate. Advisors are responsible for ensuring the research-based relationship content posted by their advisees is accurate, timely, professional, and appropriately referenced. Advisors must approve the content their advisees create before it can be posted. MyRelevate advisors may specify how many, whom, and for how long they advise.
