MyRelevate is a community of scholars preparing to deliver relevant, research-informed relationship content to young adults at the touch of their fingers. Through both website and mobile app, MyRelevate gives users the ability to have real data in the palm of their hands and on-the-go. The purpose of MyRelevate is to help single individuals and couples take the steps needed to make the best decisions pertaining to their personal development and love life. There is a desperate need to establish and maintain fulfilling romantic relationships in each of our lives. Together, we can make a lasting positive impact in the way we view love and relationships looking forward.
